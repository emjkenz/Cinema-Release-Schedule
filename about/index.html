My Movie Search

'The Movie Search' project is a search movie app that's so good, it'll make you want to grab some popcorn and settle in for a movie marathon. From searching for movies in your local area to checking out new releases, this app has got you covered.

A team of tech-savvy superheroes Chris, David and Emily brought the 'My Movie Search' project to life! They are a team you can count on to get the job done right.

Their technical skills are top-notch in HTML, CSS, Javascript/jQuery, as well as intergrating 3rd party APIs. They are always eager to learn and adapt to new technologies faster than a speeding bullet. So why settle for ordinary when you can have extraordinary?

Special thanks to code expert, Tristan Kennedy, for keeping 'The Movie Buffs' sane throughout this project. We would also like to give a special mention to our project mascot, Gizmo. Your calming purrs were appreciated.
